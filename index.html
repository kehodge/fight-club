About Fight Club

Directed by oscar nominee David Fincher, this “darkest of dark satires” starring Brad Pitt and Edward Norton. Norton gives a outstanding performance as a label-obsessed insomniac on the verge of going postal. Befriending a interesting soap salesman, they embarks on an existential crusade that takes them both to the brutal,raw heights of their insanity.

Starting a small fight club became a movement within itself and triggers man across the world to embrace their inner beast, anger and disappointment in todays society and take their life back.Stop being defined by your credit score, case number or your wealth be defined as the man you are.
